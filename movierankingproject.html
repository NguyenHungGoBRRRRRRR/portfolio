My top 3 movies of all time

The Dark Knight

A superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan, with Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger as the iconic Joker1.

The Godfather

A classic crime drama directed by Francis Ford Coppola, featuring Marlon Brando and Al Pacino. It explores the Italian-American mafia.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful drama about hope, friendship, and redemption in a prison setting. Directed by Frank Darabont, it stars Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman.
